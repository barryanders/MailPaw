MailPaw

Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent handling of your data.

What MailPaw collects

MailPaw does not collect or transmit email content. Templates, styles, and settings are stored locally in your browser on your computer so your template library stays private unless you choose to copy, export, or share it.

Payments and licensing

MailPaw is free. It does not collect payment details, run subscriptions, or require a paid account.

How data is used

  • To save templates and settings locally in your browser for privacy.
  • To export backup files that you can restore later or keep outside the browser.

Data sharing

No data is sold. MailPaw does not use analytics, ads, or tracking.
